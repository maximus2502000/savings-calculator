Privacy Policy

Short version: your numbers stay in your browser. We don't store them.

The short version

Savings Roast does not collect, store, or transmit any of the financial information you enter into the calculator. Your savings amount, spending, and income figures are used to run the calculation in your browser and are never sent anywhere.

Last updated: May 2026

What data we collect

Financial inputs: none. The savings, spending, and income numbers you enter are processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. They are never sent to our servers, stored in a database, or logged anywhere.

Usage data: none collected by us. We do not run our own analytics on this site. We do not use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any third-party tracking service. We do not set cookies.

Error logs: none. Server errors from Cloudflare Pages are handled at the infrastructure level and do not contain any user-entered data.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to run the site:

  • Cloudflare Pages — hosts and serves the website. Cloudflare may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages visited) as part of normal web hosting. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for details.
  • Google Fonts — the Inter typeface is loaded from Google's servers. This means your browser makes a request to Google when you visit the site. See Google's privacy policy for details.

That is the complete list. We do not use advertising networks, affiliate tracking pixels, social media widgets, or any other third-party scripts.

Cookies

Savings Roast does not set any cookies. The only cookies that may be present are those set by Cloudflare for security purposes (such as bot detection). We do not use cookies for tracking, advertising, or personalisation.

Children's privacy

Savings Roast is a general-purpose financial estimation tool. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided information through this site, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

Changes to this policy

If we add features that change how data is handled (such as email capture or a backend API), we will update this policy before those features go live and note the change date at the top of this page.

Contact
